# 📊 Telco Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior in a telecommunications company using Power BI. The goal is to identify key churn drivers, understand customer retention patterns, and provide actionable recommendations to reduce customer loss and protect revenue.

---

##  Objectives

* Identify customers at risk of churn
* Analyze factors influencing churn behavior
* Evaluate retention trends across customer segments
* Provide data-driven business recommendations

##  Key Insights

### 1. Customer Tenure (Primary Driver)

* Churn is highly concentrated among **new customers (low tenure)**
* Customers with longer tenure show significantly higher retention

Early-stage customer experience is critical to retention

---

### 2. Tech Support Impact (Strongest Driver)

* **77.37% of customers without tech support churn**
* Only **16.59% with tech support churn**

Lack of support services is the **biggest contributor to churn**

---

### 3. Service Adoption

* Customers without:

  * Online Security
  * Device Protection
  * Online Backup
    show significantly higher churn rates

Value-added services increase customer stickiness

---

### 4. Payment Method Risk

* **57.30% of churn comes from Electronic Check users**
* Other payment methods show much lower churn

Payment behavior is a key indicator of churn risk
---

### 5. Contract Type & Revenue Impact

* Month-to-month contracts:

  * Lowest average revenue (~$1.2K)
  * Highest churn risk
* Long-term contracts:

  * Higher value (~$4K–$5.4K)
  * Better retention

 Long-term customers are more profitable and stable

---

### 6. Customer Demographics

* Gender distribution is nearly equal → **not a churn driver**
* Senior citizens represent **25.5%** of customers
* Customers with partners (**35.8%**) and dependents (**17.4%**) show moderate influence

---

###7. Service Usage Insights

* Majority of customers use phone services
* Multiple line usage is evenly distributed → not a major churn factor

---

## Retention Analysis

* Retention increases steadily with tenure
* Customers in their first year are at the highest risk of leaving
* Adoption of support and security services significantly improves retention

---

##  Business Recommendations

### 1. Improve Customer Onboarding

* Focus on first 12 months
* Provide guidance and engagement programs

---

### 2. Promote Tech Support Services

* Bundle tech support with subscription plans
* Offer free trials to increase adoption

---

### 3. Encourage Long-Term Contracts

* Provide discounts for yearly or multi-year plans
* Incentivize contract upgrades

---

### 4. Optimize Payment Methods

* Reduce reliance on electronic checks
* Encourage automated and digital payment options

---

### 5. Upsell Value-Added Services

* Online security
* Device protection
* Online backup

 These services increase customer retention and lifetime value

##  Conclusion

This analysis reveals that customer churn is primarily driven by low tenure, lack of support services, and specific behavioral factors such as payment method and contract type. By focusing on early customer engagement and enhancing service value, businesses can significantly reduce churn and improve revenue retention.
