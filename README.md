# FUTURE_DS_02

# Telco Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior in a telecommunications company using Power BI. The objective is to identify key drivers of customer churn, evaluate retention trends, and provide actionable recommendations to reduce customer loss.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze customer churn patterns
* Identify high-risk customer segments
* Understand factors influencing churn
* Provide data-driven recommendations for retention

* Customers at Risk:1,869

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Customer Tenure

* Customers with shorter tenure show significantly higher churn rates
* Long-term customers are more stable and valuable

---

### 2. Tech Support Impact

* Majority of churn comes from customers **without tech support**
* Customers with tech support show significantly lower churn

---

### 3. Service Adoption

* Customers without **online security, backup, and device protection** contribute highly to churn
* Value-added services improve customer retention

---

### 4. Internet Service

* Fiber optic users form the largest customer segment
* Churn within this segment poses a high revenue risk

---

### 5. Customer Demographics

* Gender does not significantly influence churn
* Senior citizens represent a smaller but important segment

---

## Retention Analysis

* Retention improves as customer tenure increases
* Early-stage customers (first year) are the most at risk of churn

---

##  Business Recommendations

### 1. Improve Customer Onboarding

* Focus on engagement during the first 12 months
* Provide guidance and support for new users

---

### 2. Promote Tech Support Services

* Offer bundled plans including tech support
* Provide free trials to increase adoption

---

### 3. Upsell Value-Added Services

* Encourage adoption of online security, backup, and protection
* Increase perceived value of services

---

### 4. Target High-Risk Customers

* Customers with short tenure
* Customers without support services
* Customers with high monthly charges

## 🚀 Conclusion

This analysis highlights that customer churn is largely driven by low tenure, lack of support services, and limited service adoption. By focusing on customer experience and value-added services, businesses can significantly improve retention and reduce revenue loss.
